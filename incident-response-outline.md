# Incident Response Outline – Phishing Attack Scenario

## Objective
To outline a basic response plan for handling a phishing incident in a healthcare environment.

---

## Incident Scenario
An employee clicks a phishing email and unknowingly provides login credentials.

--

## Response Steps

### 1. Detection
- Identify unusual login activity  
- User reports suspicious email  

---

### 2. Containment
- Disable compromised account  
- Isolate affected systems  

---

### 3. Eradication
- Remove malicious email from systems  
- Reset passwords  

---

### 4. Recovery
- Restore secure access  
- Monitor for further suspicious activity  

---

### 5. Lessons Learned
- Conduct staff awareness training  
- Improve email filtering systems
